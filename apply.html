Pilot

Our pilots are world class aviators that are held to the highest standards of any commercial airline. We take pride in our pilots and want capable and attentive pilots. Our pilots are to be respectful and have a certain amount of experience. If you are looking for a job that is easy and will make you a lot of money quickly, this is not the job for you. This job is for strong-willed individuals that take pride in their job and will work towards and stay with their job for years.

Flight Attendant

Flight attendants are the first person a passenger sees when entering the plane. He/she is there to greet the passengers and serve them throughout the flight. The flight attendant represents our airline as much as our planes do. When a passenger flies, our flight attendants is what makes them want to fly a with our airline again. We put our flight attendants through a four week class that teaches them how to peform their job in a classy and efficient manner. Being a flight attendant is a job for strong-willed and respectful people who can peform their job efficiently even under stress.
